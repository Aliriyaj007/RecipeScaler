User Guide

Getting Started

RecipeScaler helps you adjust recipes for any number of servings. Simply paste your recipe, set your desired scaling, and get perfectly adjusted ingredient amounts.

Basic Features

  • Paste Recipe: Copy-paste any recipe into the input box
  • Automatic Parsing: Click "Parse Ingredients" to identify amounts
  • Adjust Servings: Use the slider or presets to scale the recipe
  • View Results: See original and scaled recipes side-by-side

Enhanced Features

  • Unit Conversion: Switch between Imperial and Metric systems
  • Custom Multipliers: Enter exact scaling factors
  • Export Options: Copy, print, or export your scaled recipe
  • Theme Selection: Choose from 7 color themes

Advanced Features

  • Shopping List: Generate consolidated ingredient lists
  • Pan Calculator: Adjust baking pan sizes appropriately
  • Custom Settings: Toggle display preferences and behaviors
  • Import/Export Config: Save and restore all settings

Tips & Tricks

  • Recipes parse best when ingredients are on separate lines
  • Use the "¬Ω Recipe" preset for testing new recipes
  • Toggle between fractions and decimals in Settings
  • Export your configuration to use on another device

RecipeScaler is a client-side tool. All processing happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
